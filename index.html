Meet the trainer behind the treats

Hi, I'm Chloe, a positive reinforcement dog trainer, passionate about helping dogs and their owners build lasting, trust-based relationships.

My training methods are force-free, reward-based, and adapted to suit your dog's personality. Whether you're working on puppy foundations, lead skills, recall, reactivity, confidence building, or muzzle training, I'm here to support you every step of the way.

My dog, Zuri, is the reason behind everything I do. Her fear reactivity led me to the world of dog training, and through that journey, we built a deeper connection. Training not only helped her, it strengthened our relationship, helping us become best friends.

I offer one-on-one dog training and hope to one day expand my business to include a secure dog field and an indoor training space, providing a safe and enriching experience for every dog.

Let's train with love and kindness, because good relationships start with trust.

I am a registered dog trainer with the Animal Behaviour & Training Council (ABTC), with over six years of hands-on experience working with and training dogs through two of the UK's largest animal welfare charities.

My qualifications include:
  • Canine Behaviour Professional - Accredited Diploma
  • Canine Reactive Behaviour - Accredited Advanced Certificate
  • CPD Certified - Canine Fear Course
  • Animal First Aid Trained
  • Currently studying: Level 4 Diploma in Canine Welfare, Training & Behaviour
  • Professional Member of the National Institute for Canine Ethics

With a strong foundation in both practical experience and academic learning, I am dedicated to promoting ethical, welfare-focused dog training.
